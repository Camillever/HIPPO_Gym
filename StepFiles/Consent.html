General Information

You are being asked to take part in a research study carried out by the above researchers. This form explains the research study and your part in it if you decide to join the study. Please read the form carefully, taking as much time as you need. Ask the researcher to explain anything you don’t understand.

You can decide not to join the study. If you decide to participate in the study, you can change your mind and stop at any time. There will be no penalty or loss of services/benefits if you decide to not take part in the study or quit later.

This study has been approved for human subject participation by the Washington State University Institutional Review Board [change to UofA??].

What is this study about?

This research is being conducted to study the ways a human can learn knowledge more efficiently leveraging information given by a system.
Depending on which setting you are assigned, completion of the study will take between 15-40 minutes.

Are there any benefits to me if I participate in this study?

There are no direct benefits to you from participating in this study. However, through this study, we will gain a better understanding about the benefits of how information is presented during learning.

This research could have broader educational, economical, and societal benefits as the information gained is expanded to other domains and target populations.
